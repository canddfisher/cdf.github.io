About Us

Christian and Dena met in July 2022 while Dena was travel nursing in Charlottesville, Virginia. After meeting, Dena continued travel nursing and Christian’s remote job allowed him to tag along. In 2023, Dena landed a job as an outpatient triage nurse at a level 1 trauma hospital outside of DC and Christian transitioned to a new engineering role with the Army Corps of Engineers.

Christian proposed to Dena later that year, at Tatte’s bakery and coffee shop. A year later, they tied the knot in Punta Cana, Dominican Republic! Now you’ll find them living in Reston, VA, with their two pups, Hazel Jo the wiener dog and Louis the mini bernedoodle. Together, they look forward to the adventures that lie ahead, hand in hand, with faith guiding the way.
