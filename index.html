About Us

We are Christian and Dena, two souls united in love, faith, and a shared devotion to family. Starting our journey together as husband and wife, we are excited to build a life grounded in the values we cherish — love, commitment, and the joy of everyday moments.

Christian serves as a fire protection engineer with the Army Corps of Engineers, proudly supporting U.S. interests in the Middle East. His dedication to safeguarding others extends beyond his work, always ensuring the well-being of those around him. Dena, a registered nurse, works alongside a team of surgeons, offering compassionate care to patients and supporting them through their medical journeys.

When we’re not working, you’ll likely find us enjoying long walks, spending time in nature, or simply relaxing at home with our beloved pups, Hazel and Louis. They’re a constant source of joy and companionship in our lives. Together, we look forward to the adventures that lie ahead, hand in hand, with faith guiding our way.
